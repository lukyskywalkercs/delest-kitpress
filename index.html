BIO

DELEST is a melodic punk rock band formed in 2015 in Castelló, Spain, with influences stemming from Californian punk rock. Our musical career is backed by 5 albums, numerous concerts across Spain, and 2 international tours in England. This doesn't even count the extensive previous experience of each member in other bands. "PERSISTENCIA" is our latest album. If you're interested in booking us for your festival or venue, please get in touch. We're confident we can work out an arrangement. We are supported by 20 Chords Records, an independent music label based in Barcelona with an impressive roster of underground bands.

"PERSISTENCIA" - NEW EP

"PERSISTENCIA" is our new EP. It was recorded, mastered, and produced by Raúl Artana at Rockaway Studios (Castellón, Spain) and mixed and arranged by Angus Cooke (Santa Barbara, California), known for his work with top melodic punk rock acts like Lagwagon, Bad Astronaut, and The Ataris. The EP features 4 tracks brimming with melodic punk rock, where octaves, backing vocals, and strong vocal melodies take center stage. In "PERSISTENCIA," we delve into the relentless journey that has taken its toll on us, making our bones feel heavier and our hinges creak. Yet, we haven't stopped striving to be what we aim for: a punk rock band that channels its raw energy, leaving every last drop of sweat on stage at each show, and collecting bruises in this vast sea of music. This is PERSISTENCIA, our new EP. We hope you enjoy it as much as we do. Please share it and, above all, continue to support the punk rock scene.
